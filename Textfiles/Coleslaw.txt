Name:
Coleslaw
Categories:
Coleslaw, Cabbage, Salad, Lactose free, Gluten free
Time:
10 min
Servings:
4 (500 grams)
Ingredients:
1 cabbage
1 carrot
2 green onions
4 tablespoons mayonnaise (egg)
1 teaspoon yellow mustard
2 teaspoons wine vinegar
0.2 teaspoon black pepper
Phases:
1. Place the thinly sliced cabbage, carrot, and onions in a large bowl.
2. Add the dressing ingredients and gently mix so that all of the shredded cabbage is coated with the dressing.
3. Taste and add more salt and pepper if needed. Serve immediately.
